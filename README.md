# Milly's date invitation 💌

A single-file interactive invitation. To use:

1. **Get a shareable link** → go to https://app.netlify.com/drop and drag `index.html` onto the page. It gives you a public URL instantly.
2. **Send it** → paste that link into your Instagram DM to Milly.
3. **(Optional) tweak copy** → open `index.html`, find the `CONFIG` block near the top of the `<script>` to change names/options, or edit the card text further down.

No accounts, no server, nothing to maintain. When she finishes, the final card lets her **copy her answers** (or screenshot the itinerary) and paste them straight back into your DM.
