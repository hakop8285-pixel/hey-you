# Milly's date invitation 💌

A single-file interactive invitation. To use:

1. **Edit `index.html`** → find the `CONFIG` block near the top of the `<script>` and set `YOUR_PHONE` to your real mobile number. Tweak any copy you like.
2. **Test it** → double-click `index.html` to open it in your browser and click through the whole flow.
3. **Get a shareable link** → go to https://app.netlify.com/drop and drag `index.html` onto the page. It gives you a public URL instantly. Text that URL to Milly.

No accounts, no server, nothing to maintain. When she finishes, her answers open a pre-filled text (or email) to you — she just hits send.
